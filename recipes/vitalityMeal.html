Vitality Meal

Healthy food isnt always a bad taste

This meal will aid you on hitting your daily protein target which will make you big and strong.

Ingrediants

  • Organic Steak
  • Organic Avacado
  • 2 Organic Dates
  • Organic Almonds
  • Black pepper
  • Pink himalayan sea salt

Steps

  1. Step one grab your chopping board and prepare your avacado.
  2. Put your almonds in a water mixture with some ACV to detoxify them.
  3. Put your Iron pan to heat 4 and add coconut oil to prevent burning and sticking.
  4. After 3 minutse swap the steaks side.
  5. When the steak is ready season it.
  6. Add all ingrediants on a plate together and enjoy the mixture.
